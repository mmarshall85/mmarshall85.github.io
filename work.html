The care for our planet is ruled by the way we treat ourselves.  At the core of conscious treatment of the self is how we feed our mind, our bodies and our spirit. The nourishment we get from food and the other products we ingest into our bodies through our daily routine are our focus. Halevai and Kollectiv are working with product makers with long term true sustainability goals and creating a show room for the positive communication around these products. We are co-developing several brick and mortar locations for access to these products, services and nourishment. In addition to this face to face in person experience we are growing the virtual capacity of the products and services we sell so that we can share with a larger demographic who may not be able to experience us in store.

 

Halevai is working with Soel Yachts on developing a creative narrative and guide path to expansion of their highly evolved, highly engineered motor yachts. We will perform  a financial underwriting by the Steering committee on Soel Yachts. Set up personal meetings between the Halevai advisors, steering committee and outside financial partners, bank and institutions. Halevai will create a 12-48 month communications plan and opening of new key distribution markets for Soel Yachts. This will ensure that the programs are implemented with the appropriate level of visibility geared towards the right intended audience to ensure distribution into new markets.

 

To create a steering committee for the Island of Barbados built on opening up discussions and collaborations around innovation policy, product development, bio technology integration, clean manufacturing, waste management, education  and communication around new forms of commerce and industry for the Island of Barbados and the greater Caribbean. Our Steering committee is made up of seven members from Halevai, Barbados business owners and entrepreneurs, Environmental Agency and University Department Heads.

 

Halevai X MEDSEA are working together on an applied research study program. Our program goal is to create a wide spread aggregated scientific network of data collected throughout the Mediterranean, Atlantic coast lines of Europe and Africa, South Africa, the Atlantic of US and Canada, across the Caribbean and US Atlantic and Gulf coastal zones. We are most interested in understanding Estuarine habitats. These habitats include salt marshes, mud flats, tidal streams, tidal waterways, lagoons, reefs and corals, barrier beaches, and rocky intertidal shores. Our study of these critical marine ecosystem(s) is vital to,  and a real time indicator of, overall marine health systems. We are interested to expand this cooperative network of scientists and institutions to better understand these systems and believe that by building a strong geographical understanding of these environments we can develop narratives for product concepts who can give drive a communication around the importance of these environments as well as drive a vehicle of revenue to support the existing efforts of the collaboration program.

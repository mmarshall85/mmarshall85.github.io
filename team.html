OUR TEAM

FRANK HEIDINGER

Founder

 

Frank Heidinger is an ocean lover, explorer, producer and entrepreneur. He was on the founding team that created Parley for the Oceans, an environmental organization and global collaboration network focused around ocean fragility issues. He is the creative director of the Fari Islands Foundation. Frank has worked extensively on international brand licensing throughout Europe, Asia and the Caribbean.

ALAIN PALINSKY

Founder

 

For more than 25 years Alain has been a consumer of health and wellness products. He attended a professional sports academy and studied abroad in Europe. Stepping away from finance in 2008 he made choose to focus on all things Wellness. He is a co-founder of Juice Press and founder of Kollectiv a wellness concept store. He also holds a law degree from Moscow legal academy.

EVAN LICHT

Managing Director

 

Inventive executive with 25+ years experience leading high growth and start-up companies in the consumer, media and technology sectors. Prior experience includes senior leadership roles at eLance (Upwork), SonicNet (MTVi) and most recently as CBDO for Blade Urban Air Mobility

MIKE MARSHALL

Managing Director

 

Originally from Toronto, Canada, Mike Marshall has lived and worked for the last 15 years in New York. His background has been focused in hospitality and marketing and has worked with clients to bring their vision to life in a way that authentically connects with the audience.

LETITIA GARRIOT DE CAYUEX

Advisory Board

 

Letitia Garriot de Cayuex is an entrepreneur and investor passionate about innovation and STEM, creating impact at scale, forging a more diverse and inclusive future and foreign affairs. She serves as a Board Member of the National Museum of Mathematics and a member of the Council on Foreign Relations. She is an early investor in US transformative technology champions including SpaceX and SparkCognition. She co-founder of Escape Dynamics.

Dr. Ayana Elizabeth Johnson PH.D

Advisory Board

 

Dr. Ayana Elizabeth Johnson is a marine biologist, policy expert, writer, and Brooklyn native. She is founder and CEO of Ocean Collectiv, a strategy consulting firm for conservation solutions grounded in social justice, and founder of Urban Ocean Lab, a think tank for the future of coastal cities. With Dr. Katharine Wilkinson, she co-edited the anthology All We Can Save, and co-founded The All We Can Save Project. Recently, Ayana co-created the Blue New Deal, a roadmap for including the ocean in climate policy. For three years, she taught a seminar on urban ocean conservation as an adjunct professor at New York University.

GEOFFREY HESS

Advisory Board

 

Geoffrey is an international specialist and head of perpetual for the Americas at Phillips Auction House. He is the former CEO of Analog/Shift and remains on their advisory board today. He is an Attorney by trade and was senior advisor to former Mayor Rudy Giuliani and then headed Giuliani Partners when the mayor returned to the private sector.

HANDE SADIC

Advisory Board

 

Hande Sadic is a multi-disciplined Executive with 20 years of industry experience of leading and executing fully integrated operation & supply chain strategies for the international retail and luxury brands. She holds expertise in developing and executing big picture product road maps and ensuring the initiation of product development strategy to full completion; overseeing all product life cycle/end-to-end production in implementing operational and branding strategies for contemporary brands.

STEVEN KLEIN

Advisory Board

 

Steven is an American Artist. Susan Bright, Curator of the National Portrait Gallery’s “Face of Fashion” exhibition says of Klein, "His Collaboration is all about confidence building, so that by the time it comes to taking the picture, everyone knows exactly what is going to happen. Klein is not only a portraitist of the biggest names and most pivotal moments on the conveyor belt of fame; he creates iconic images that sell."

RICHIE AKIVA

Advisory Board

 

A native New Yorker, Richie Akiva’s passion has always been entertainment.  His roots were sown deep up in NYC’s Tribeca neighborhood, where he began producing events in his teenage years, developing a reputation as a savvy entrepreneur with a magnetic personality. After graduating Northeastern University, Akiva found his true passion for event production and opened several restaurants and entertainment venues including Butter Restaurant with Iron Chef and renowned Food Network star Alex Guarnaschelli.  He has shifted his focus toward philanthropy in the past 10 years and has co-produced some of the worlds largest annual social fundraisers, AmFar, Leonardo DiCaprio Foundation Unicef and Rihanna's Diamond Ball.

FRED GRIMMEL

Advisory Board

 

Fred Grimmel is a Los Angeles based hospitality curator. He works with some of leading  groups in the US. Growing up he had a passion for music and was an accomplished drummer. As a performer he understood Artist Hospitality and has developed strong relationships with some of Hollywood's most relevant artists and musicians. This led to curating high profile events for corporate clients and artist tours. Fred prides himself on relevancy, understanding and identifying emerging artists, trends and new ways of connecting with the public. Fred has supported local organizations in charity and fundraising efforts by donating venues, providing musical talent and sponsors for causes including the Parley for the Oceans, Born X Raised Sadie Hawkins, Bet Tzedek's Justice Ball and local COVID-19 Relief Efforts

DANIEL BRAVER

Advisory Board

 

Daniel Braver has immersed himself in health and wellness for the last 16 years. He holds a clinical doctorate in physical therapy, is a NYS licensed massage therapist, a corrective exercise specialist and a nutrition zealot. He brings a wide breadth and depth of knowledge along with a deeply rooted compassion to his clients. He is currently DPT in residency.

PETER GURNZ

Advisory Board

 

A native New Yorker.  A pioneer of industrial design and photography, Gurnz’s photographs come framed and constructed with resin, acrylic, wood, metal, glass (known as “Emulsions,”) which have a beauty of imagery and structural design that give a nod to the classic stain glass windows of the renaissance era. A relationship between Form and Function. His are works placed in the permanent collections of the Guggenheim, and many private collections. His ambitious creations marry the form of photograph with the functional construction of the physical piece. In this digital age of intangible media where the feel of holding a photograph between fingertips has been forgotten, Gurnz is able to capture and carry a story of a 2D photograph and thousand different ways.
